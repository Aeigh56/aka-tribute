LONG LIVE SUPERMEGA

(28/January/1988-10/February/2023)

Who He Was

Kiernan Jarryd Forbes, professionally known as AKA, was a South African rapper, record producer and businessman. AKA was born in Cape Town and lived with his grandmother in Mitchells Plain. He later moved to Johannesburg in 1995 with his mother Lynn Forbes. AKA spent his teenage years at St John's College, where he formed a hip-hop group named Entity with his group members Vice Versa & Greyhound. Kiernan was the oldest of two boys, hes younger brothers name being Steffan Forbes.

About his Life

AKA was a very well known musician whose life basically could not shy away from the media. He dated some of Mzantsi's well known celebrities. including Bonang Matheba, DJ Zintle as well as his final girlfriend having been the Zimbabwean-South African rapper, Nadia Nakai. On the 8th of July 2015, AKA and DJ Zintle welcomed their baby-girl Kairo. AKA was known to be a very affectionate and loving individual around those he loved.

His Music Career

As much as AKA's career began during his teenage years when he was part of a HIP-HOP group, his fame did not begin the. In 2009 was when AKA ventured ito a solo career. His career took off as he released hit singles such as Problems, In My Walk and Do It. Although, it was only after his debut-album, Alter-Ego that he rose to Stardom. He has won countless awards during his career and has been name as one of the greats in the game.

How he died

AKA was in Durban as he was booked to perform. He was unfortunately assasinated outside a restuarant as he was leaving.
